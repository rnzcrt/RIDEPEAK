At RidePeak, we believe every ride should be an adventure—whether you're conquering a mountain trail, racing on open roads, or cruising through the city. Our passion for biking drives us to craft high-performance bikes that meet the demands of all riders, from seasoned pros to everyday commuters.

Founded on the principles of quality, innovation, and sustainability, RidePeak bikes are designed to handle the toughest challenges while offering comfort, style, and durability. We understand that every cyclist has unique needs, which is why we offer a wide range of bikes—from rugged mountain bikes to sleek road bikes, versatile hybrids, and agile BMX models.

We're more than just a bike company—we're a community of enthusiasts dedicated to promoting a healthy, active lifestyle. Our mission is to inspire people to embrace the freedom of cycling and explore the world on two wheels.

Join us, and let's ride to the peak—together.

RidePeak was born in the heart of Colorado in 2010, inspired by the breathtaking Rocky Mountains and the thriving outdoor community. Our founders, a group of cycling enthusiasts, sought to create bikes that could handle the diverse terrain they loved to explore—from steep mountain passes to smooth city roads. With backgrounds in engineering and design, they combined their expertise to craft bikes that were not only built for performance but also for the love of the ride.

Starting out in a small garage, we've grown into a global brand, but our passion for adventure and craftsmanship remains unchanged. Every RidePeak bike is still meticulously designed to give you the ultimate riding experience, no matter where your journey takes you.
